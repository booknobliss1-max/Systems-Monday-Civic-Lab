About the Civic Lab

The Systems Monday Civic Lab is a civic-tech learning space that transforms public meetings—like the Dallas City Council Transportation & Infrastructure Committee—into reusable civic intelligence. Each lab session documents, structures, and publishes insights that strengthen community systems and transparency.

Our Workflow

  • Attend: Observe public meetings and civic processes.
  • Document: Capture structured notes and decisions.
  • Synthesize: Translate raw data into clarity-layer artifacts.
  • Publish: Share civic intelligence through dashboards and archives.

Featured Projects

Transportation & Infrastructure Dashboard: A clarity-layer visualization of Dallas City Council committee actions.
